Relocation of the LE-Network office

LE-Network moved to a new location on 1 March 2017. The new address is:

Dordsestraat 33
7811 LA Emmen
The Netherlands

The new location is on the 2nd floor above the Heeroma Zorgwinkel.
This can be achieved by taking the stairs up in the care store and then at the end of the hall to the left of the stairs. At the location there is possibility to park, the location is also easily accessible by public transport with a short walk from the Center.
